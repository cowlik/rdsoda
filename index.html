About

RdSoda's concept resonates with me on a personal, nostalgic level...

Back in the day, when life was simpler and more innocent, one of the best things about skateboarding to me was the road trips. Sometimes planned... sometimes spontaneous, these trips would consist of hitting the road with the crew, heading up to amazing events such as Slam City Jam, stopping at numerous skateparks, and a lil' bit of mischief.

Armed with one or two disposable cameras, only a handful of photos would remain along with some scattered memories. I always wished there was a way to visually document those trips... a way to share it with family and friends so they could watch it happen in real-time. Well, hopefully this application serves as a great way to showcase your traveling experiences. Check out the "How-To" section below to get started.

- Clint

How-To

So you've decided to come along and have a RdSoda, huh? Well, to get you started, you'll need to have a few things:

First and foremost, have an Instagram account that is public.

Second, have some sort of adventure planned where you actually cover some ground (goin' from downstairs to upstairs doesn't count).

Thirdly, give that adventure a title and make sure it contains any of the following words:

  • "adventure"
  • "tour"
  • "trip"
  • "vacation"
  • "viaje"

ex: "thegreatestroadtripofalltime"

Ok, now take that first photo around the starting point of your trip, then:

  1. Upload the photo to your Instagram account.
  2. Make sure it is Geotagged.
  3. Hashtag it with the title of your trip. ex: #tourdebrewery
  4. Finally, stamp it with a #rdsoda hashtag and submit it!

Repeat steps 1-4 after you take that next photo.

As you take more photos, you'll see your trip unfolding and being documented.

PRO TIP: You can also link directly to your trip so your friends and relatives can follow along. Just click on your trip and select the appropriate sharing application.
